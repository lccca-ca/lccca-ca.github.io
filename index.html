Case-Specific Research and Analysis

Dr. Ariana Mohammadi is an expert consultant in forensic and legal linguistics and is a licensed legal practitioner. She thoroughly reviews legal cases in which linguistic evidence plays an important role. Using advanced tools and methodologies in applied linguistics, corpus linguistics, and sociolinguistics, she collects and analyzes linguistic data and provides confidential, yet independent and impartial, written reports. When linguistic evidence contributes to your theory of the case, she works within the criteria set out in R v Mohan to provide expert reports and testimony and help you build a strong legal case. Dr. Mohammadi works with small and large law firms, corporations, and organizations in the public and private sector and provides expert witness services throughout Ontario and at all court levels.

Our Services

Case Review and Analysis

If language plays a role in a legal matter and can be presented to the court as evidence, we analyze the evidence for merits and focus on determining the significance of the linguistic evidence in the case.

Confidential Verbal Consultation

We provide impartial expert opinion in a confidential manner. While our expert opinion is based on linguistic scientific facts, our analysis of the case is further informed by the pertinent Rules of Evidence.

Custom-Curated Written Report

We provide specialized, custom-curated expert reports for both plaintiffs and defendants. Our expert reports meet the requirements set out in Rule 53.03 of the Ontario Rules of Civil Procedure.

Expert Witness Testimony

We provide expert witness testimony at all court levels, including federal and provincial courts and tribunals. We participate in trial testimony anywhere within the province of Ontario.
